Welcome to Kings!

So you want to play but have no cards. Never Fear!

Dont know Kings? Learn about the game here

Press on the card or the "Draw" button to draw a card from a randomly shuffled deck. The card number will be shown and the rule displayed with it.

To see the rules, or to customise to your own liking click the "Show Rules" button and modify any rule(s) to your own houserules.

To start over, simply click the "Reset Deck" button and the deck will be reset and reshuffled.

On mobile? Click the "Fullscreen" button for a more immersive experience!
